My Dog-Blog!

About this blog!

This blog was inspired by me and my family recently concluding with what dog breed was our favorite. Personally I am a cat-person anyway but when it comes to breeds I have more knowledge of dogs.

There is no real purpose to the blog other than sharing my personal favorite dog-breeds. I am incredibly biased toward bigger dogs, who can be useful, and I also have this innate feeling that smaller dogs are dumber than larger dogs.

And as you were probably all wondering, it is time for the big reveal! What dog-breed did me and my family conclude as our favorite? You guessed it! The Toller! (Blog post #10)
